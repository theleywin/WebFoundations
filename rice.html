Creamy Rice Pudding

Let´s go!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups cold water

  • ¾ cup uncooked white rice

  • 2 cups milk, divided

  • ⅓ cup white sugar

  • ¼ teaspoon salt

  • 1 large egg, beaten

  • ⅔ cup golden raisins

  • 1 tablespoon butter

  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation

  • Step 1
    • 
            Pour water into a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat; stir in rice.
            Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and liquid has been absorbed, about 20 minutes.
  • Step 2
    • 
            Combine cooked rice, 1 ½ cups milk, sugar, and salt in a clean saucepan.
            Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until thick and creamy, about 15 minutes.
  • Step 3
    • 
            Stir in remaining 1/2 cup milk, beaten egg, and raisins; cook 2 minutes more, stirring constantly.
            Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla until combined; serve warm.
